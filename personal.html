GEYSER INSURANCE

We cover loss of or damage to your buildings caused by the bursting of geysers or hot water cylinders. We also cover the damage to the geysers from as little as R40pm.

Our cover is subject to the following conditions. The geyser must have been:

  • Manufactured in accordance with the South African Bureau of Standards (SABS) approved standards
  • Installed by a qualified person registered with the Plumbing Industry Registration Board (PIRB) or any similarly recognised authority in accordance with the standards set by South African National Standards (SANS).
