Why do you need legal insurance?

There are many events that could potentially incur expensive legal costs, such as criminal, civil, labour or matrimonial matters. Your cover affords you access to legal advice as well as legal representation when you need it most – 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Your cover also includes access to customised legal documents, such as finalising contracts for your domestic employees or potential divorce proceedings.
