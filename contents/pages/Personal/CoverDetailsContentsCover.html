Did you know?

With Household Contents Insurance, you will be protected by the insurance company that has paid 99% of claims and is perfect for peace-of-mind.

Do you qualify?

1.

You should be residing in a house

2.

The house should have brick walls

3.

By law, you are required to have a valid ID Number and be 18 years or older

4.

Your house should have a valid address along with an active phone number

5.

You should have a valid & active bank account

Theft

Key benefits

Theft & damage

Items of yours as well as those who live with you will be covered in the instance of theft or damage.

Coverage of your items

In the instance of theft, your items will be covered to maximum of 10% of the insured value and may be claimed twice a year.

Large appliance cover

Appliances such as your TV, washing machine and fridge will be covered for natural perils as well as theft.
