Did you know?

Santam’s home and household insurance was designed with you in mind. You may cover both your house and your belongings, so if anything goes wrong, we will repair your home or replace your possessions to the value that you are insured for. It is affordable, beneficial and it would give you peace of mind.

Do you qualify?

1.

You should be the owner of the house

2.

The house should have brick walls

3.

By law, you are required to have a valid ID Number and be 18 years or older

4.

Your house should have a valid address along with an active phone number.

5.

You should have a valid & active bank account.

Lightning

Storms

Key benefits

Structural damage

The structure of your home will be covered for loss & damage in the case of a natural disaster.

Liability cover

We will cover you if there is injury or death to persons on your insured property based on the limit you have agreed upon.

Natural disaster

Boundary walls along with outer buildings will be covered in the instance of a natural disaster if they are part of the registered address.
