Did you know?

With Micro SME Insurance, your business will be protected from theft, fire, damage and natural disasters, thus protecting your business from financial instability.

Do you qualify?

1.

You should be 18 years or older

2.

You should have a valid South African ID or be in possession of a valid SA work permit

3.

Your business should have a valid address

4.

You should be a holder of a valid active bank account

5.

You should have an active and valid phone number

Key benefits

Office equipment

This is cover for equipment that are used for the operation of your business. E.g – Cash drawers, light fixtures and chairs.

Materials in trade

This is cover for items that are used for your trade. E.g – Moulds, designs and documentation.

Tools

This is cover for hand-held tools that contribute to the operation of your business. E.g – Hammers, saws & toolboxes.

Machinery

This is cover for your devices that contribute toward a service you offer along with its parts. E.g – Grinders, tile-cutters and drills.

Glass

This is cover for any glass within your registered business address. Includes windows etc.

Electronic equipment and for business interruption

This is cover for your generators etc. in the case of power outages.

Sasria

This is cover for accidental or intentional damage during uprisings, strikes and riots.

Flat excess

With a flat excess rate, you will always know what your excess is and it will not be calculated based on a percentage.
