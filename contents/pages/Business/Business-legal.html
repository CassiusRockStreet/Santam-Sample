Santam Legal Insurance

Santam Legal Insurance is insurance that is designed with the individual and small and medium-sized business owner in mind. This legal expense insurance is tailor-made to provide you with cover against potential legal costs and expenses brought against you by another individual or a business on a wide range of legal disputes. Legal insurance offers you the peace of mind that you have access to the best legal counsel should you require it.

Why should you have Legal Insurance?



Santam business legal cover is a legal expense insurance specifically designed to provide cover for small to medium sized businesses against potential legal costs and expenses brought against a business or incurred during business operations.

The Santam business legal cover comprises of a unique IT system that enables a specialised offering. The integration of technology and insurance has been developed to deliver a captivating offer to the end-user.

What can businesses get with legal insurance?

Unlimited access to legal advice and representation in connection with:

  • Legal proceedings related to employee disputes
  • Civil action taken by the insured in respect of performance under contracts and due care owed to the insured
  • Arbitration and mediation related to interpretation of contracts and disputes arising there from
  • Representation to licensing authorities as a result of the insured’s licence being suspended, revoked or altered

Customised legal drafting and contracts tailored to each policyholders needs:

  • Delivered immediately after client’s online interview
  • Completed and ready for signature on delivery
  • Service reporting and BI included
  • Stored and managed via administrative console

What you receive with Legal Insurance

Santam business Legal Insurance clients will receive unlimited access to Legal advice and representation in connection with.

Legal proceedings related to employee disputes.

Actions against employees.

Arbitration and mediation related to interpretation of contracts and disputes arising therefrom.



Civil action taken by your insured business in respect of performance under contracts and due-care owed to the insured business.

Representation to licensing authorities as a result of the insured business' licence being suspended, revoked or altered.

Although there is no limit to the number of times a business can claim during an annual period or during the life of the policy, cover is limited to the insured value per annum.
